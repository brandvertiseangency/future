# Acceptable Use Policy (draft — legal review)

**Effective:** upon publication  
**Applies to:** all use of Brandvertise AI products and services (the “Services”), including AI-assisted text and **image generation**.

Brandvertise provides tools for **lawful brand and marketing content**. You must not use the Services to create, solicit, or distribute content that violates applicable law or this policy.

## 1. Prohibited uses (non-exhaustive)

You may **not** use the Services to:

1. **Non-consensual intimate or sexual material**  
   Create, alter, or distribute imagery or video that depicts real people in intimate or sexual contexts **without their meaningful consent**, including “undressing,” “face swap onto adult content,” voyeuristic content, or similar harms.

2. **Deceptive synthetic media (“deepfakes”)**  
   Impersonate a real individual, public figure, or organization in a **misleading** way that could cause harm, fraud, reputational damage, or political disinformation. Parody and clearly labeled fiction are not a workaround for fraud or harassment.

3. **Child sexual abuse or exploitation**  
   Any sexualization of minors, or content that facilitates child exploitation, is strictly forbidden and will be reported to authorities where required.

4. **Illegal violence or terror content**  
   Content that incites, instructs, or glorifies **serious real-world violence** or terrorism.

5. **Harassment, hate, and discrimination**  
   Targeted harassment, slurs, or content attacking people based on protected characteristics, when tied to real harm.

6. **Intellectual property and trademark abuse**  
   Use third-party marks or copyrighted works **beyond what you have rights to use** for your campaign (you remain responsible for clearing rights to reference images and briefs).

7. **Malware, spam, or fraud**  
   Phishing, malware distribution, bulk unsolicited messaging, or deceptive commercial practices.

## 2. Brand and reference content

You represent that you have the **rights and permissions** needed for any logos, product photos, or people’s likenesses you upload or describe. Brandvertise may refuse or remove generations that appear to violate this policy or applicable law.

## 3. Enforcement

We may **block generation**, **remove content**, **suspend or terminate accounts**, and **preserve records** as described in our internal procedures and applicable law. Blocking by automated filters is not a complete guarantee of legality or safety; you remain responsible for your use.

## 4. Reporting

See [reporting-and-contact.md](./reporting-and-contact.md) for how to report suspected abuse. We aim to **acknowledge** reports within **72 hours** and triage according to severity (faster for high-risk categories such as NCII).

## 5. Changes

We may update this policy. Continued use after notice constitutes acceptance of the updated terms where permitted by law. Material changes may require **re-acknowledgement** in the product.
